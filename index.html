Dr. Alix Bjorklund

Dr. Alix Bjorklund has been in private practice in Santa Fe New Mexico since 1993.

"I utilize the traditions and wisdom of Chinese and Asian Medicine, including Acupuncture, Five Element Theory, Chinese herbology, and Massage; while also utilizing modern biomedicine techniques. Qi, Inc. has a large pharmacy of Chinese herbs and supplements. I specialize in women's health, autoimmune disorders, oncology, and spiritual emotional health; in addition to treating everything from allergies to sports injuries. I am here to guide you through nutrition and lifestyle counseling, stress management, mindfulness and meditation coaching. These are all integral aspects of your treatment plan.

My studies of Taoism, Buddhism, meditation, mindfulness, and psychology guide my practice. These interest have led me to travel in Burma, China, Cambodia, Laos, Thailand and Japan. When I am not at the clinic, I am outdoors exploring the mountains, I have a profound love of nature. Loving kindness, compassion and living in harmony are the guiding principles that direct my daily life and practice."
- Dr. Alix Bjorklund

Qi, Inc. offers Massage, Reike and Sound Healing with Holistic Healthcare Practitioner, Ilseanel Gonzalez, LMT.
